Canon PIXMA TR4520 Wireless All-in-One Printer

The Canon PIXMA TR4520 is a compact and affordable wireless inkjet all-in-one printer designed for home offices and personal use. Offering printing, scanning, copying, and faxing capabilities, it delivers high-quality results for everyday tasks.

Key Features

  • Print Resolution: Up to 4800 x 1200 dpi for sharp text and vibrant photos.
  • Print Speed: Approximately 8.8 ipm (images per minute) for black and white documents; 4.4 ipm for color documents.
  • Automatic Duplex Printing: Save paper with automatic two-sided printing.
  • Wireless Connectivity: Supports Wi-Fi, Apple AirPrint, Mopria Print Service, and the Canon PRINT app for easy mobile printing.
  • Automatic Document Feeder (ADF): 20-sheet capacity for efficient scanning and copying.
  • Voice-Activated Printing: Compatible with Amazon Alexa for hands-free printing commands.

Design and Build

The PIXMA TR4520 features a compact design, measuring 17.2" x 11.7" x 7.5" and weighing approximately 13 pounds, making it suitable for small spaces. The front panel includes a 1.9" LCD display and a set of physical buttons for easy navigation. While the build quality is functional, the display may be challenging to read from certain angles due to its fixed position.

Performance

While not the fastest printer in its class, the TR4520 offers reliable performance for low to moderate printing volumes. It produces high-quality prints with sharp text and vibrant colors, making it suitable for documents, photos, and creative projects. The 20-sheet ADF simplifies multi-page scanning and copying tasks, enhancing productivity.





Software and Mobile Printing

Canon provides a comprehensive software suite, including Scan Utility, Easy-PhotoPrint Editor, and My Printer, for both Windows and Mac users. The Canon PRINT app enables wireless printing and scanning from smartphones and tablets, offering added convenience. Additionally, the printer supports voice-activated printing through Amazon Alexa, allowing users to initiate print jobs using voice commands.

Cost Efficiency

The PIXMA TR4520 uses standard and high-yield ink cartridges, offering options to suit different printing needs. While the printer's running costs are higher compared to some competitors, its affordable upfront price and versatile features make it a cost-effective choice for users with moderate printing requirements.

Conclusion

The Canon PIXMA TR4520 Wireless All-in-One Printer is a reliable and feature-rich device suitable for home offices and personal use. With its compact design, wireless connectivity, and high-quality output, it provides a convenient solution for everyday printing, scanning, copying, and faxing tasks.
