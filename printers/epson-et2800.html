Epson EcoTank ET-2800 Wireless Color All-in-One Cartridge-Free Supertank Printer with Scan and Copy

The Epson EcoTank ET-2800 is a compact and efficient wireless inkjet all-in-one printer designed for home offices and small businesses. Offering printing, scanning, and copying capabilities, it delivers high-quality results for everyday tasks.

Key Features

  • Print Technology: Inkjet
  • Print Speed: Up to 10 pages per minute (black), up to 5 pages per minute (color)
  • Print Resolution: Up to 5760 x 1440 dpi
  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi, USB 2.0
  • Duplex Printing: Manual two-sided printing
  • Mobile Printing: Apple AirPrint, Google Cloud Print, Mopria Print Service, Epson iPrint app
  • Paper Handling: 100-sheet input tray, 30-sheet output tray
  • Display: 1.44-inch color LCD screen




Performance & Quality

The Epson EcoTank ET-2800 delivers impressive print quality with resolutions up to 5760 x 1440 dpi, ensuring sharp text and vibrant photos. Its manual duplex printing feature allows for efficient two-sided printing, reducing paper usage. The printer's compact design and lightweight build make it an ideal fit for small home offices or personal use.

Connectivity & Compatibility

With multiple connectivity options, including Wi-Fi and USB 2.0, the ET-2800 offers flexible integration into various setups. The Epson iPrint app enables remote printing and scanning from smartphones and tablets, providing added convenience for users on the go.

Cost Efficiency

Utilizing Epson's high-yield ink bottles can significantly reduce printing costs. The printer's low cost per page for black-and-white printing makes it an economical choice for users with high-volume printing needs. The 100-sheet input tray and 30-sheet output tray minimize the need for frequent paper refills, further enhancing efficiency.

Conclusion

Overall, the Epson EcoTank ET-2800 Wireless Color All-in-One Cartridge-Free Supertank Printer is a reliable and feature-rich device suitable for home offices and small businesses. Its combination of high-quality printing, efficient duplex capabilities, and cost-saving features makes it a valuable addition to any workspace.
