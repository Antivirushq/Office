Brother MFC-J1010DW Wireless Color Inkjet All-in-One Printer

The Brother MFC-J1010DW is a compact and affordable wireless inkjet all-in-one printer designed for home offices and small businesses. Offering printing, scanning, copying, and faxing capabilities, it delivers high-quality results for everyday tasks.

Key Features

  • Print Technology: Inkjet
  • Print Speed: Up to 9.5 pages per minute (black), up to 6.0 pages per minute (color)
  • Print Resolution: Up to 4800 x 1200 dpi
  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi, USB 2.0, Ethernet
  • Duplex Printing: Automatic two-sided printing
  • Mobile Printing: Apple AirPrint, Google Cloud Print, Mopria Print Service, Brother iPrint&Scan app
  • Paper Handling: 150-sheet input tray, 20-sheet automatic document feeder (ADF)
  • Display: 1.8-inch color LCD screen




Performance & Quality

The Brother MFC-J1010DW delivers impressive print quality with resolutions up to 4800 x 1200 dpi, ensuring sharp text and vibrant photos. Its duplex printing feature allows for efficient two-sided printing, reducing paper usage. The 20-sheet ADF simplifies scanning and copying multi-page documents, enhancing productivity.

Connectivity & Compatibility

With multiple connectivity options, including Wi-Fi, USB 2.0, and Ethernet, the MFC-J1010DW offers flexible integration into various setups. The Brother iPrint&Scan app enables remote printing and scanning from smartphones and tablets, providing added convenience for users on the go.

Cost Efficiency

Utilizing Brother's high-yield ink cartridges can significantly reduce printing costs. The printer's low cost per page for black-and-white printing makes it an economical choice for users with high-volume printing needs.

Conclusion

Overall, the Brother MFC-J1010DW Wireless Color Inkjet All-in-One Printer is a reliable and feature-rich device suitable for home offices and small businesses. Its combination of high-quality printing, efficient scanning, and cost-saving features makes it a valuable addition to any workspace.
