HP Envy Inspire 7955e All-in-One Printer

The HP Envy Inspire 7955e is a versatile all-in-one inkjet printer designed for home and small office use. Combining high-quality photo printing, efficient scanning, and convenient copying capabilities, it caters to a wide range of everyday printing needs.

Key Features

  • Print Technology: Inkjet
  • Print Speed: Up to 13 pages per minute (black), up to 10 pages per minute (color)
  • Print Resolution: Up to 4800 x 1200 dpi
  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi, USB, Ethernet
  • Duplex Printing: Automatic
  • Mobile Printing: HP Smart app, Apple AirPrint, Mopria-certified
  • Paper Handling: 125-sheet input tray, 15-sheet photo paper tray, 35-sheet automatic document feeder (ADF)
  • Display: 2.7-inch color touchscreen

Performance & Quality

The HP Envy Inspire 7955e delivers impressive print quality with resolutions up to 4800 x 1200 dpi, ensuring sharp text and vibrant photos. Its duplex printing feature allows for efficient two-sided printing, reducing paper usage. The 35-sheet ADF simplifies scanning and copying multi-page documents, enhancing productivity.

Connectivity & Compatibility

With multiple connectivity options, including Wi-Fi, USB, and Ethernet, the Envy Inspire 7955e offers flexible integration into various setups. The HP Smart app enables remote printing and scanning from smartphones and tablets, providing added convenience for users on the go.

Cost Efficiency

Utilizing HP's Instant Ink subscription service can significantly reduce printing costs. By subscribing to HP Plus during setup, users receive six months of free Instant Ink, allowing for predictable monthly printing expenses and fewer cartridge replacements.

Conclusion

Overall, the HP Envy Inspire 7955e All-in-One Printer is a reliable and feature-rich device suitable for home and small office environments. Its combination of high-quality printing, efficient scanning, and cost-saving features makes it a valuable addition to any workspace.
